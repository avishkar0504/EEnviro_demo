Welcome to EXCELLENT ENVIRO

Founded in 2011, EXCELLENT ENVIRO has diversified interests in Water and Waste Water Treatment Projects, including Designing, Supply, Erection, Commissioning & Operation and Maintenance of the Treatment Plants. We also deal in statutory documentation like Procurement of Consent, Submission of Environmental Statements and Environmental Monitoring. We offer Consultancies in the field of Environment, Hazardous Waste Transpiration, Solid Waste / Resource Management & Safety. The company believes on policy & integrity with the concept of “Sustained Development” & carries out all our business with total consciousness & trust. We have corporate mission for conservation of all resources & maintaining high quality standards in delivering our services or products.
