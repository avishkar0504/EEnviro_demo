Company Profile

Our Activities

Discover the comprehensive range of environmental services we offer.

At **“Excellent Enviro”**, our full-fledged **Project Division, Laboratory, and Techno-legal teams** are dedicated to addressing environmental challenges with increasing awareness and stringent statutory compliance requirements. We are actively engaged in the following key areas:

Sewage / Effluent Treatment Plants & Common Effluent Treatment Plants

  • Conducting Treatability / Feasibility Studies on Effluent Streams and finding out most economical techno-commercial solutions.
  • Designing, Construction / Supply, Erection & Commissioning of Sewage Treatment Plants, Industrial Effluent Treatment Plants & Common Effluent Treatment Plants, on **Turn-key Basis**.
  • Basic & Detailed Engineering: Environmental Projects.
  • Project Management on Environmental Projects: Procurement Assistance, Project Schedules, Supervision during project execution, Supervision during plant start up.
  • Performance Audits of STPs, ETPs & CETPs for judging adequacy / inadequacy of units & equipment in the plants.
  • Operation & Maintenance of Sewage / Effluent Treatment Plants & CETPs on Contract Basis with experienced man-power.
  • Trouble-shooting of STPs, ETPs & CETPs.
  • Providing expertise services for Operations of ETP / STP on monthly Retainer-ship basis.

Filtration & Other Tertiary Treatment Units

We offer supply, installation/erection, operation, and maintenance services for a range of advanced treatment systems:

  • Pressure Sand Filters & Gravity Sand Filters.
  • Activated Carbon Filters.
  • Demineralization Water Plant.
  • Water Softeners.
  • RO Plants (Reverse Osmosis).

Laboratory and Research & Development Services

Our state-of-the-art laboratory and R&D division offer precise analytical and preparatory services:

  • Potable Water, Ground Water & Wastewater (Effluent/Sewage) – Sampling & Analysis.
  • Fugitive & Process Emission Monitoring.
  • Flue Gas analysis from Stacks.
  • Noise level monitoring.
  • Light & Illumination surveys.
  • Hazardous Waste & Solid Waste Characterization.
  • Soil Analysis.
  • Characterization of Wastewater.
  • Treatability studies.
  • Preparation of feasibility reports.
  • Base-line & Post Environmental Clearance studies.

Other Techno-legal Environmental Services

We assist industries in navigating complex environmental regulations:

  • Assist in granting Consent to Establish/Operate/Renewal/ Amendments of Consent.
  • Periodic Statutory Report Submission.
  • Follow-ups & Liaison with CPCB / MPCB and other Statutory authorities.
  • Preparation of Environmental Statement as per Statutory requirements.
  • Preparation of Environmental Management Plan & Gap Analysis.
  • Undertaking Effluent Treatment Plant Performance Efficacy Audit.
  • Undertaking Legislative Compliance Audit.
  • Comprehensive & Rapid Environmental Impact Assessment of Industrial & Other Infrastructure Projects.
  • Preparation of Detailed Project Report.
  • Assist in Procurement of Statutory Environmental Clearances from MoEF.

Hazardous Waste, Bio-Medical Waste, MSW & E-Waste

We provide end-to-end solutions for various waste streams:

  • Management, Handling, Transport & Disposal of Hazardous Waste.
  • Management, Handling, Transport & Disposal of Bio-Medical Waste.
  • Management, Handling, Transport & Disposal of Municipal Solid Waste.
  • Comprehensive Waste Management / Resource Management in Industries.

Training

We conduct specialized / tailor-made Training Programs for Industrial Personnel as well as General Public on following topics:

  • Environmental Awareness.
  • Statutory Record-keeping and Periodic Report submission, for Industry.
  • Statutory Compliance Requirements.
  • Training for Operators / Shop-floor Personnel on Operations / Monitoring of Pollution Control Facilities.
  • Laboratory Analysis.
  • ISO 14000 – awareness, Gap-analysis etc.
